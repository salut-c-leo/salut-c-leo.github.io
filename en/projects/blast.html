Description


  • Solo
  • 1 month
  • Game Jam
  • Godot

In A Blast is a 1st-person exploration game in which you play as Raymond, a factory worker, trying to prevent the resell of his workplace which would have disastrous consequences.

Project breakdown

I made this game on my own in the context of the Fuck Capitalism Jam 2024, a month long game jam with the goal of creating an anticapitalism-themed piece of media. I went with the theme of sabotage, as it is an uncommon way of protesting, while mentioning its not completely positive consequences in the context of the game's universe. As the jam was a month long, it was additionally an opportunity to improve my documentation skills, so I spent the first week writing.

Positive points:

  • I can make a game entirely on my own with Godot
  • Feedback from other participants are positive (solo project, narrative, level design...)

What I could improve on:

  • Better management of my time: I spent too much time on buildings and structures that are not explorable, with some that even didn't make it to the final release. This could have been avoided, have I dedicated time to make a priority list and setting deadlines.
